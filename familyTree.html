Family Tree

January 24th, 2016

This is the Angelov Family Tree. It includes my immediate family as well as my dog, Goldy and my grandparents. I was born in Bulgaria and my family and I moved to the US in 1997. I attempted incorporate the theme of travel in my family tree by tying family members together with a dotted line while laying my family tree a top a map. My grandparents are positions in Europe since that is where they were born and spent most of their lives. On the other hand, my parents, who were the first in my family to move to the US, are portrayed as bridging the gap between Europe and the US. My brother and I as well as Goldy consider our home to be in the US therefore we are placed to the west. Halos were drawn overhead the family members who have passed away and hearts between those who are married. A gradient was added to the background to give the effect of an ocean further emphasizing the map like structure of my family tree.
