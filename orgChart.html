Organizational Chart

January 24th, 2016

This organizational chart depicts a simplified company hierarchy. Each level of the hierarchy was color coded to separate the positions based on their distance from the President. Furthermore, the boxes encapsulating the individual positions were given a shadow in order to make the organizational chart stand apart from the background. The background was given a linear gradient to compliment the color scheme for the individual positions. A crystalline effect was added to the background to give depth to the piece.
